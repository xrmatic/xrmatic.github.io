# Support for Bookmarks Reel

Thank you for using **Bookmarks Reel**. We are committed to providing a private, high-performance visual experience for your bookmarks. 

If you have any questions, encounter issues, or would like to suggest new features, please don't hesitate to reach out.

## How can we help?

### 🛠 Issues & Bug Reports
If something isn't working as expected, please include the following in your email to help us troubleshoot:
* Your version of Chrome.
* A brief description of the steps to reproduce the issue.
* Any specific error messages you encountered.

### 💡 Feature Requests
Have an idea to make Bookmarks Reel better? Whether it's a new layout option, advanced filtering, or a change to the capture logic, we value your feedback and look forward to hearing your suggestions.

### ❓ General Questions
Not sure how the storage caps work or how to configure the idle threshold? Check the **Extension Options** page first, or send us an email for clarification.

---

## Privacy & Security Reminder
As documented in our [Privacy Policy](privacy.html), **Bookmarks Reel does not collect or transmit any of your data.** All screenshots, metadata, and settings are stored locally on your device in `chrome.storage.local`. When you email us for support, please do not include any sensitive or personal information from your bookmarks.
